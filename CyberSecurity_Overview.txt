Cyber Security Overview

Cyber security is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. 
It focuses on maintaining confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.

In cloud storage systems, security becomes more critical because data is stored remotely and accessed over the internet.

Common threats include:
- Malware attacks
- Unauthorized access
- Data breaches

Proper security measures such as encryption, authentication, and access control are essential.
